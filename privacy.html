Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: May 16, 2025  ·  Effective: May 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the PayProTec Partner Portal — a web application used by authorized PayProTec partners to manage merchant accounts and send account communications.

1. Who We Are

PayProTec ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") operates the PayProTec Partner Portal, accessible at portal.mypayprotec.com. We provide payment processing services and a management platform for our authorized partner network.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@mypayprotec.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and credentials used to log in to the Partner Portal.
  • Merchant data: Business names, merchant IDs, contact details, processing volumes, and account status for merchants associated with your partner account. This data originates from PayProTec's payment processing systems.
  • OAuth tokens: When you connect a Gmail or Microsoft Outlook account, we store encrypted access and refresh tokens in our database. These tokens allow the Portal to send emails on your behalf.
  • Usage data: Actions taken within the Portal such as notes added, tickets created, and emails sent. We do not track browsing behavior or use third-party analytics.
  • Communications: Content of messages sent through the Portal's messaging system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating your access to the Partner Portal.
  • Displaying merchant account data associated with your partner relationship.
  • Sending emails to your merchants on your behalf using your connected Gmail account — we do not send emails from our own servers on your behalf without your explicit action.
  • Enabling internal communications between partners and the PayProTec team.
  • Supporting ticket submissions and equipment management requests.
  • Maintaining security logs and preventing unauthorized access.

4. How We Use Google User Data

When you connect your Gmail account to the Partner Portal via Google OAuth:

  • We request access to the gmail.send scope only, which allows us to send emails from your Gmail account.
  • We request access to your email address (userinfo.email) to display which account is connected.
  • We do not read, store, index, or analyze your Gmail messages, contacts, or any other Gmail data.
  • Your OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before being stored in our database.
  • Emails sent through the Portal are sent by you, from your account, to your merchants. PayProTec does not retain copies of these emails.
  • You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting your Gmail from the Settings page, or by visiting your Google Account Permissions.

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database hosting) and Vercel (application hosting) to operate the Portal. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Business operations: Merchant data within the Portal is accessible to PayProTec staff for operational and support purposes.

6. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • All OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Partner sessions use cryptographically random tokens with automatic expiry.
  • Access to merchant data is scoped strictly to the partner's own merchant portfolio.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your partner account is active. OAuth tokens for connected email accounts are retained until you disconnect the account or until the token is automatically revoked. Merchant data is retained in accordance with PayProTec's standard data retention practices.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Accessing the personal information we hold about you.
  • Requesting correction of inaccurate data.
  • Requesting deletion of your data (subject to operational requirements).
  • Revoking OAuth access at any time through the Settings page or your Google Account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mypayprotec.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active partners of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date.
